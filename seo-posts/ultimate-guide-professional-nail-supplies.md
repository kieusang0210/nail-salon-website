## 1. The Core Product Lines: Gels, Acrylics, and Dipping Powders

To satisfy a modern clientele, your salon must offer a versatile menu of services. Delivering flawless nails requires specialized, high-performance product systems designed for durability and ease of application.

### A. Professional Gel Systems
Gel manicures remain the most requested service in salons nationwide due to their glossy finish and chip-resistant wear. To deliver an outstanding gel service, your inventory should include:
*   **High-Performance Base & Top Coats**: Formula matters. Opt for high-bonding base coats that prevent lifting and scratch-resistant, non-wipe top coats that lock in glass-like shine.
*   **Pigmented Professional Gel Polishes**: Curate a rich spectrum of highly pigmented colors that apply smoothly without shrinking or wrinkling under UV/LED light.
*   **Builder & Hard Gels**: Indispensable for overlays and lightweight extensions, allowing technicians to sculpt natural-looking, ultra-strong nail enhancements.

![Professional Gel Systems Flat Lay](/assets/gel-systems.jpg)
*Figure 2: A curated selection of premium gel polishes and swatch charts.*

### B. High-Strength Acrylic Systems
Acrylics are the foundation of nail extensions, offering unmatched durability and length customization for clients who love structured nail designs. A premium acrylic setup requires:
*   **Fine-Milled Acrylic Powders**: Stock up on bubble-free, non-yellowing powders in core shades like crystal clear, soft pinks, deep cover nudes, and bright whites.
*   **Advanced Liquid Monomers**: Choose standard or low-odor monomers formulated with UV inhibitors to protect color clarity and ensure a smooth, controllable curing rate.
*   **Dehydrators & Acid-Free Primers**: Essential prep steps that maximize adhesion and prevent moisture buildup or lifting.

![Acrylic Nail Application Close-up](/assets/acrylic-application.jpg)
*Figure 3: Sculpting detailed acrylic extensions with a professional brush.*

### C. Healthier Dipping Powder Systems
Dipping powder systems continue to grow in popularity as a durable, vitamin-infused alternative for clients seeking strong nails without UV light exposure. Your dipping inventory needs:
*   **Essential Prep & Finish Liquids**: High-quality bonders, base liquids, activators, and sealers to ensure clean layer building and rapid drying.
*   **Finely Ground Dipping Powders**: Richly pigmented, finely milled powders that apply evenly for a lightweight, natural feel that lasts up to four weeks.

## 2. Professional Tools and Equipment

Beyond polishes and acrylic powders, nail salon equipment plays a massive role in service efficiency, safety, and technician comfort.

### A. Electric Nail Drills (E-Files)
A high-quality, low-vibration E-File saves valuable time during prep work, shaping, and product removal. 
*   **What to look for**: Choose a brushless motor with adjustable speeds up to 35,000 RPM, high torque, and a quiet, heat-dissipating handpiece. Cordless, rechargeable models are ideal for maintaining a clean, wire-free workstation.

![Cordless E-File and Drill Bits](/assets/efile-drills.jpg)
*Figure 5: High-precision electric nail drill with diamond bit attachments.*

### B. Smart LED/UV Curing Lamps
Efficient curing prevents service delays and product lifting.
*   **What to look for**: Invest in smart LED/UV lamps with at least 48W of power, 360-degree bulb placement to eliminate blind spots, and motion sensors. Removable reflective base plates are crucial for sanitary pedicure applications.

![Modern LED Curing Lamp](/assets/led-curing-lamp.jpg)
*Figure 6: A client curing a fresh gel manicure inside a sleek LED lamp.*

### C. Precision Implements & Sanitization Systems
Client safety and hygiene are non-negotiable. Protect your business and clients with:
*   **Medical-Grade Stainless Steel Implements**: Cuticle nippers, pushers, and nail clippers that hold their sharp edges through repeat autoclave cycles.
*   **Single-Use Disposables**: Premium nail files, buffers, and wooden sticks to ensure a completely sanitary experience for every individual client.
*   **Autoclave Sterilizers & UV Cabinets**: Cleanliness is the best marketing tool. Openly display your sterilization protocols to build client trust.
