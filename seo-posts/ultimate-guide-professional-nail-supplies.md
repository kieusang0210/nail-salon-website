## 1. The Core Product Lines: Gels, Acrylics, and Dipping Powders

To cater to a diverse clientele, your salon must offer a complete range of services. Each service requires specialized, high-performance product systems.

### A. Professional Gel Systems
Gel manicures remain the most popular service in modern salons due to their durability, high gloss, and quick curing time. To offer top-tier gel services, you need:
*   **Base and Top Coats**: High-bonding base coats and scratch-resistant, high-shine top coats (non-wipe formulas are highly recommended).
*   **Gel Polishes**: A rich palette of highly pigmented gel colors that apply smoothly and do not shrink under LED/UV lights.
*   **Builder Gels & Hard Gels**: Essential for overlays and lightweight extensions that mimic the natural nail.

### B. High-Strength Acrylic Systems
Acrylic nails are the backbone of nail extensions, offering unmatched durability and length customization. A high-quality acrylic setup includes:
*   **Acrylic Powders**: Fine-milled powders in various shades (clear, pink, white, nude, and cover powders) that prevent bubbles and yellowing.
*   **Monomer Liquid**: Standard or odorless liquid monomers with UV inhibitors that ensure optimal curing speed and color stability.
*   **Prep Products**: Acid-free primers and dehydrators to prevent lifting.

### C. Healthier Dipping Powder Systems
Dipping powder (SNS and other brands) is a popular alternative for clients seeking strong, long-lasting color without UV curing. Essential components:
*   **Bond, Base, Activator, and Sealer**: The multi-step liquid system required to build and lock the dipping powder.
*   **Dipping Powders**: A wide variety of shades that are finely ground for a lightweight, natural feel.

## 2. Professional Tools and Equipment

In addition to chemicals and polishes, your technicians need reliable, ergonomic tools that deliver precision and withstand continuous sterilization.

### A. Electric Nail Drills (E-Files)
A high-torque, low-vibration E-File is indispensable for prep work, backfills, and removing extensions. Look for models with adjustable speeds up to 35,000 RPM, brushless motors for quiet operation, and rechargeable portability.

### B. LED/UV Curing Lamps
Invest in smart LED/UV lamps with high wattage (at least 48W) and 360-degree bulb placement to ensure even curing. Removable bottom plates are essential for pedicures and sanitation.

### C. High-End Implement and Sanitization Equipment
*   **Cuticle Nippers, Pushers, and Scissors**: Made of medical-grade stainless steel that remains sharp after autoclave sterilization.
*   **Disposable Files, Buffers, and Pumice Stones**: To maintain strict hygiene and build trust with clients.
*   **Autoclave Sterilizers and UV Sanitizers**: Critical for meeting health regulations and guaranteeing safety.
