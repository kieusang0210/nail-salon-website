Our Story

Welcome to Luxe Nail Bar & Spa

At Luxe Nail Bar & Spa, we believe every client deserves beautiful nails and a relaxing spa experience. Founded in Austin, TX, our luxury salon provides high-end manicures, organic spa pedicures, precise acrylic sets, lightweight Gel-X systems, and highly creative custom nail art in a friendly, sparkling clean environment.

Whether you're celebrating a bridal event, popping in with friends for a weekend pedicure treat, or treating yourself to a fresh gel manicure after work, our dedicated team is committed to making you look and feel your absolute best.
